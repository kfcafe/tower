Products and practice

Use the products or work directly with me.

The site should not hide one side behind the other. Arcana needs to read as both a real product company and a strong studio for teams building similar systems.

Products

Use the stack directly.

The product side is for developers and teams who want runtimes, orchestration, and better command surfaces for working with agents.

  • Open foundations imp and mana stay free, composable, and useful on their own.
  • Convenience on top Wizard and Familiar package the workflow into something easier to adopt and live in.
  • Trust as part of the product Review, verification, and durable work state are not afterthoughts.
Consulting

Bring Arcana in for the hard parts.

The consulting side is for teams building agent products, internal workflow systems, or developer tools that need more than a thin model wrapper.

  • Product shaping Clarify what should be automated, supervised, sold, and measured.
  • System architecture Design control surfaces, failure handling, approvals, and review paths.
  • Implementation Go from rough prototype to something that can survive real users and real teams.
