Products

Use the stack directly.

  • Open core foundations imp and mana stay free and composable.

How I work

Technical, opinionated, and close to the code.

The consulting side should feel like an extension of the product work: pragmatic, architecture-aware, and willing to get specific. Not generic AI transformation copy.

01 — Shape

Find the real product surface.

Clarify what needs to be automated, what must stay supervised, and which workflow is actually worth turning into a product instead of a one-off demo.

02 — Build

Design for trust, not just output.

Agent loops need review, explicit gates, recovery paths, and operator visibility. I care as much about failure modes and control surfaces as I do about generation.

03 — Ship

Turn prototypes into systems people can live with.

That means stronger interfaces, better defaults, clearer product framing, and enough engineering quality that the result survives contact with real users.
