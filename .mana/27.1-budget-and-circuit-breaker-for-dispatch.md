Add cost ceiling and consecutive-failure circuit breaker to PoolConfig and the dispatch loop.

PoolConfig gains:
- max_cost: Option<f64> — stop dispatching after cumulative cost exceeds threshold
- max_consecutive_failures: u32 — halt after N failures in a row (default 0 = disabled)

Dispatch loop tracks running cost total from AgentResult.cost and consecutive failure count. Emits new PoolEvent variants (BudgetExceeded, CircuitBroken) when limits hit.

Tests: budget_stops_after_cost_exceeded, circuit_breaker_halts_on_consecutive_failures, circuit_breaker_resets_on_success.
